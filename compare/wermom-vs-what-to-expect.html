Wermom vs What to Expect: Which Pregnancy & Baby App Is Best in 2026?

Wermom and What to Expect are both pregnancy and baby apps, but they take fundamentally different approaches. What to Expect is built on the famous book series and provides extensive, week-by-week pregnancy and baby guides, community forums, and milestone tracking. It's content-rich with generic information curated by experts. Wermom focuses on personalized health tracking with AI-powered assessments, customized reports, meal planning, sleep coaching, and growth analysis from pregnancy through age 6. Here's how they compare.

Content vs. Personalization: The Core Difference

This is the fundamental distinction. What to Expect is primarily a content platform—you read expert-written articles, guides, and week-by-week information. Wermom is a personalization platform—it analyzes your unique situation and generates customized recommendations and reports.

What to Expect approach: "Week 20 of pregnancy: Here's what's happening with your baby, common symptoms, what to expect this week." Generic but comprehensive.

Wermom approach: "You're at week 20, and based on your health assessment showing vegetarian diet + sensitivity to nausea + preference for natural remedies, here's a personalized nutrition plan and sleep improvement suggestions." Specific to your situation.

Both provide valuable information, but in different ways. What to Expect is like having a library of parenting books. Wermom is like having a personal health advisor analyzing your specific data.

Pregnancy Guides & Week-by-Week Content

What to Expect Strengths: Exceptional week-by-week pregnancy guides covering what's happening with your baby, common symptoms, when to call a doctor, and what to expect. The content is authoritative (based on the famous book series) and extensive.

Wermom: Provides pregnancy information, but it's integrated into the health tracking platform rather than standalone articles. Pregnancy content is personalized based on your health assessment.

If your primary need is reading detailed pregnancy guides, What to Expect excels. If you want personalized pregnancy guidance alongside health tracking, Wermom integrates both.

Baby Tracking & Health Monitoring

Wermom: Comprehensive baby tracking covering sleep, feeding, symptoms, moods, growth, development milestones. The app analyzes patterns and provides AI-powered recommendations. For example: "Your baby's sleep improved when you adjusted bedtime by 30 minutes, suggesting a circadian rhythm issue."

What to Expect: Milestone tracking and baby guides. You can log milestones and check them off. Content includes when babies typically smile, crawl, talk, etc. Less focused on continuous health tracking.

For hands-on health monitoring and pattern analysis, Wermom is stronger. For milestone benchmarking and general baby information, What to Expect is solid.

Community & Forums

What to Expect: Active, large community with forums covering pregnancy, parenting, baby health, and life as a new parent. You can ask questions and get peer advice.

Wermom: Limited community features. Primarily a one-to-one health tracking relationship between you and the app.

If community support is important to you, What to Expect is significantly better. If you prefer personalized health insights without peer forums, Wermom is focused.
