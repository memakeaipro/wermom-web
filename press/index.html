About Wermom

Wermom is a comprehensive, AI-powered baby health platform designed to support mothers from pregnancy through their child's sixth year. By combining advanced artificial intelligence with medical expertise, Wermom provides personalized health guidance, real-time symptom assessment, and evidence-based parenting support—transforming how families manage their children's health and wellbeing.

Our platform serves over 30,000 active mothers who rely on Wermom's 93-question expert assessment, 570+ educational articles, and AI-personalized meal plans. With a 4.9-star rating across 50,000+ reviews, Wermom has become the trusted health companion for modern families seeking reliable, accessible guidance during critical developmental stages.

Built by a team of pediatric experts, AI specialists, and experienced parents, Wermom bridges the gap between pediatric care and everyday parenting. We empower mothers with instant, personalized health insights while maintaining the highest standards of medical accuracy and data privacy.

Core Capabilities

  • 93-Question Expert Health Assessment: Comprehensive, AI-guided symptom evaluation providing instant health insights
  • Daily Health Reports: Personalized daily wellness summaries based on your child's unique health profile
  • AI-Powered Meal Plans: Customized nutrition recommendations for babies, toddlers, and pregnant mothers
  • 570+ Educational Articles: Evidence-based parenting guidance covering every developmental stage
  • Real-Time Symptom Tracking: Monitor and log symptoms with AI-powered guidance and recommendations

Leadership & Medical Advisory

Founder & CEO

Wermom's founder is a healthcare technology innovator with 15+ years of experience building consumer health platforms and a deep commitment to maternal and child health. Driven by personal experience and a passion for democratizing healthcare, the founder created Wermom to solve the gap between expert pediatric care and everyday parenting support.

Medical Advisory Board

Wermom's content and AI models are developed under the guidance of board-certified pediatricians, pediatric nutritionists, and maternal health specialists. Our Medical Advisory Board ensures all recommendations meet the highest standards of clinical accuracy and align with current evidence-based pediatric guidelines from organizations including the American Academy of Pediatrics (AAP).
