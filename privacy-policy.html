Privacy Policy — Wermom

Last updated: March 25, 2026

Overview

Wermom is a baby and maternal health tracking application. We take your privacy seriously, especially given the sensitive nature of health data for you and your baby.

Information We Collect

We collect several categories of information to provide you with personalized health tracking. Your account information includes your email address (used for authentication) and your full name (optional, for personalization). We also collect baby information including baby's name, date of birth or due date, gender, blood type, and birth weight and height measurements.

We track comprehensive health and wellness data through your daily logs of feeding, sleep, bowel movements, mood, and symptoms. We also record growth measurements including weight, height, and head circumference. For maternal health, we collect information about blood pressure, weight, water intake, and energy levels. Additionally, we track contraction timing and kick counting data during pregnancy, and you can upload photos as moments within the app.

To enable our service features, we collect device information including push notification tokens and device type and OS version for app compatibility purposes.

How We Use Your Data

Your data enables us to provide personalized health tracking, displaying your baby's health trends, growth charts, and daily summaries. We use your logged data with our AI assistant to provide personalized nutrition and health advice. We send daily reminders, vaccination schedules, and milestone alerts through notifications, and we generate daily, weekly, monthly, and yearly health reports for your review.

Data Storage & Security

All data is stored securely on Supabase (PostgreSQL) with Row Level Security (RLS) enabled to ensure that only you can access your information. Authentication tokens are stored in your device's secure keychain or keystore using expo-secure-store. Data is encrypted in transit using TLS/HTTPS. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. Photos are stored in encrypted cloud storage.

Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You can request a full data export at any time from Settings. You can request account deletion, which permanently removes all your data within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

While this app tracks information about children (babies), the account holder must be a parent or legal guardian age 18 or older. We do not knowingly collect information directly from children.

Third-Party Services

We use Supabase for our database and authentication services (see supabase.com/privacy). Expo is used for app build and push notifications (see expo.dev/privacy). We also use an AI Service where chat conversations are processed by our AI provider to generate responses. Conversations may be stored for quality improvement but are not linked to your identity.

Your Rights

You have the right to access your personal data, correct inaccurate data, delete your account and all associated data, export your data in a portable format, and opt out of push notifications.

Health Disclaimer

Wermom is NOT a medical device and does NOT provide medical advice. The information provided by the app, including AI-generated responses, is for informational and tracking purposes only. Always consult your pediatrician or healthcare provider for medical decisions regarding your baby or your own health.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact

For privacy-related questions or data requests, contact us at privacy@wermom.app or support@wermom.com.
