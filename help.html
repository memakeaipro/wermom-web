Getting Started

Creating an account is easy! Visit our homepage and click "Sign Up." Enter your email address, create a secure password, and follow the verification steps. Once verified, you can start using Wermom right away. If you already have an account, simply click "Log In" instead.

Account & Billing

You can upgrade or downgrade your plan anytime from your account settings. Go to "Subscription" in your account dashboard and select the plan you'd like to switch to. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate any charges based on your billing cycle.

Yes, you can cancel anytime from your account settings. There are no cancellation fees or long-term contracts required. Your access continues through the end of your current billing period. We'd love to hear your feedback if you choose to leave—you can share your thoughts in the cancellation survey.

We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Discover), as well as digital payment methods like PayPal and Google Pay. Your payment information is processed securely through encrypted channels and is never stored on our servers.

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on annual plans. If you're not satisfied with Wermom within 30 days of purchase, contact our support team at support@wermom.com for a full refund, no questions asked. For monthly plans, refunds are available within 7 days of your billing date.

You can update your billing address, payment method, and other account details in the "Billing" section of your account settings. Changes take effect immediately for future charges. If you need help updating your information, our support team is happy to assist.
