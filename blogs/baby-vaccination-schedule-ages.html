What Is the Recommended Vaccination Schedule for Infants?

The CDC recommends vaccinations starting at birth (Hepatitis B), with additional vaccines at 2, 4, and 6 months including DTaP, polio, pneumococcal, and rotavirus. By 12 months, babies receive MMR and varicella. This schedule protects against 14 serious diseases before age 2.
