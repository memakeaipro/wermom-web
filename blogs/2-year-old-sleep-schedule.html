What Does a Healthy 2-Year-Old Sleep Schedule Look Like?

A 2-year-old needs 11-14 hours of sleep daily, typically 10-12 hours at night and 1-2 hours for a single afternoon nap. Most 2-year-olds do well with a bedtime between 7-9 PM and wake around 6-8 AM with one nap around 12-2 PM.
