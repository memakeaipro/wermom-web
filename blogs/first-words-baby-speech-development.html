At What Age Do Babies Usually Say Their First Words?

Most babies say their first recognizable word between 12-18 months, though some start as early as 9 months or as late as 24 months. Early communication includes cooing, babbling, and gestures. Boys often talk later than girls. Lack of any sounds by 12 months warrants evaluation.
