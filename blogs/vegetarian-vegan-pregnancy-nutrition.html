Can I Follow a Vegetarian or Vegan Diet While Pregnant?

Yes, vegetarian and vegan diets support healthy pregnancies with proper planning. Ensure adequate protein (beans, tofu, nuts), iron (legumes, fortified cereals), vitamin B12 (supplements or fortified foods), calcium (dairy or fortified plant milk), and DHA (algae supplements). Work with a dietitian for optimization.
