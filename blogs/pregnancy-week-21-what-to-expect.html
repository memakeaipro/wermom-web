Pregnancy Week 21: What to Expect, Symptoms & Baby Development

Quick Answer: At week 21 of pregnancy, your baby is growing rapidly with important developmental milestones. Your body is adapting to support this growth with various physical and hormonal changes.

Baby Development at Week 21

During week 21, your baby undergoes significant developmental changes. The neural tube, organs, and skeletal system continue forming and maturing. Each week brings new milestones in your little one's growth journey inside the womb.

Common Symptoms at Week 21

At week 21, you may experience various symptoms as your body adapts to pregnancy. These can include fatigue, nausea, food aversions or cravings, and body aches. Every pregnancy is different, so your experience may vary from others.

Tips for Week 21 of Pregnancy

Stay hydrated, maintain gentle exercise routines, and keep up with your prenatal appointments. Week 21 is a good time to discuss any concerns with your healthcare provider and ensure you're getting proper nutrition for both you and your growing baby.
