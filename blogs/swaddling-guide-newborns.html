Swaddling Your Newborn: Benefits, Techniques & Safety

Quick Answer: Understanding your baby's sleep needs and establishing healthy sleep habits is one of the most important aspects of early parenting. This guide provides evidence-based strategies.

Understanding the Science

Baby sleep patterns differ significantly from adult sleep. Understanding sleep cycles, circadian rhythm development, and age-appropriate sleep needs helps parents set realistic expectations and make informed decisions about sleep strategies.

Practical Strategies That Work

Evidence-based approaches to improving baby sleep include consistent routines, appropriate sleep environments, and age-appropriate interventions. Every baby is unique, so finding what works for your family may require trying different methods.

Common Challenges & Solutions

Sleep challenges are a normal part of infant development. From regressions to schedule disruptions, understanding why these changes happen helps parents respond effectively while maintaining healthy sleep habits long-term.
