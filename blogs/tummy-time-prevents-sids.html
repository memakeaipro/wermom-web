How Should I Do Tummy Time With My Newborn to Prevent SIDS?

Supervised tummy time during waking hours is safe and beneficial for development. Back sleeping is recommended for naps and bedtime. Tummy time strengthens muscles and varies head position, reducing flat spot risk. Never use tummy time as sleep; always supervise and gradually increase duration.
