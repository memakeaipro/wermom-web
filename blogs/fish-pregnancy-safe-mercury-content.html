Is It Safe to Eat Fish During Pregnancy? What About Mercury?

Low-mercury fish (salmon, sardines, anchovies, trout) are safe and beneficial for fetal brain development. Limit 2-3 servings weekly. Avoid high-mercury fish (shark, swordfish, king mackerel, tilefish). Canned light tuna is lower in mercury than albacore. Fish benefits outweigh risks when chosen carefully.
