Nutritional Night Feeds by Age

Research from the American Academy of Pediatrics has consistently shown that nutritional night feeds by age is one of the most impactful factors in early childhood development. Specifically, 0-3 months: 2-3 feeds. 3-6 months: 1-2 feeds. 6-9 months: 0-1 feed. 9+ months: likely habitual..

In practice, this looks simpler than you might expect. Set a daily reminder to check in on when to stop night feeds — just 2-3 minutes is enough.
