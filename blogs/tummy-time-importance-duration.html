What Is Tummy Time and How Much Does My Newborn Need?

Tummy time is supervised time with baby on their stomach, strengthening neck and shoulder muscles while reducing SIDS risk through positional variation. Start with 1-2 minutes several times daily for newborns, gradually increasing to 20-30 minutes total by 4 months. Make it playful and supervised.
