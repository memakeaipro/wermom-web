Why Is My Newborn Sleeping So Much? Is 20 Hours of Sleep Normal?

Newborns sleeping 16-20 hours daily is completely normal during the first few weeks. This extended sleep supports rapid growth and brain development. However, feed your baby every 2-3 hours even if sleeping; contact your pediatrician if baby is difficult to wake for feedings.
