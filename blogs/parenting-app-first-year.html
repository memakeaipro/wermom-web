A comprehensive parenting app combines the practical tools you need (tracking, scheduling, reminders) with the expert knowledge you crave (developmental guidance, health information, age-appropriate activities). Month-by-Month Support

The best parenting apps evolve with your baby. In months 1-3, you need robust feeding and diaper tracking with newborn-specific guidance. Months 3-6 bring sleep consolidation support and introduction to solid foods. Months 6-9 feature mobility milestones, baby-proofing guidance, and expanded nutrition tracking. Months 9-12 bring advanced developmental milestones, toddler transition preparation, and weaning support. Wermom adjusts its interface, recommendations, and content automatically as your baby grows, ensuring you always have age-appropriate tools and information.
