Frequently Asked Questions

Download the Wermom app from the App Store or Google Play. Open the app and tap "Get Started." You can sign up with your email address, Apple ID, or Google account. Follow the onboarding steps to set up your profile and add your baby's information.
You can manage your subscription through your device's app store settings. On iPhone, go to Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Wermom. On Android, go to Google Play → Menu → Subscriptions → Wermom. You can also visit our Cancellation Policy for more details.

Yes! Wermom supports tracking multiple children under one account. You can easily switch between profiles in the app. Each child has their own dedicated tracking space for feeding, sleep, diapers, milestones, and growth charts.
Go to Settings → Family Sharing in the app. You can invite your partner, family members, or caregivers by email. They'll get their own login and can view and log activities in real-time. All data syncs instantly across all connected accounts.
The free plan includes basic feeding, sleep, and diaper tracking, growth charts, milestone tracking, and daily tips. Premium features like AI-powered insights, unlimited photo storage, detailed analytics reports, and expert consultations require a Wermom Premium subscription.
Go to Settings → Data & Privacy → Export My Data. You can export your data in CSV or PDF format. This includes all tracking logs, growth data, milestones, and notes. Exports are typically generated within a few minutes and sent to your email.
Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. This will permanently remove your account and all associated data within 30 days. Backup copies are purged within 90 days. You can also request account deletion by emailing privacy@wermom.com.
