Key Pregnancy Nutrition Principles

Whatever app you choose, know these basics:

  • First trimester: Minimal extra calories (same as pre-pregnancy)
  • Second trimester: +300-350 extra calories daily
  • Third trimester: +450-500 extra calories daily
  • Critical nutrients: Folate, iron, calcium, DHA, protein, fiber
  • Foods to avoid: Raw/undercooked meat/fish, unpasteurized dairy, raw eggs, high-mercury fish, deli meats (unless heated)
