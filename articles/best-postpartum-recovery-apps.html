Top 5 Postpartum Recovery Apps Every New Mom Needs

Postpartum recovery is hard. Your body is healing, hormones are shifting, you're sleep-deprived, and you're caring for a newborn. The right apps can help you track recovery, manage pain, monitor mental health, and find support when you need it.

#1: Wermom — Baby's Health + Postpartum Tracking

Wermom

Price: $24.99/month | Features: Postpartum recovery tracking, mental health assessment, nutrition guidance for nursing mothers, baby health tracking all integrated.

Wermom is unique because it tracks both your postpartum recovery AND your baby's health. The app includes postpartum-specific mental health screening, physical recovery tracking, and nutrition guidance if you're breastfeeding.

Rather than switching between a maternal health app and a baby health app, Wermom keeps everything in one place. Your mental health data can be contextual with your baby's sleep (sleep deprivation affects mood) and feeding success (breastfeeding challenges affect postpartum depression risk).

#2: Pelvic Floor First — Physical Recovery

Pelvic Floor First

Price: Free with premium | Features: Pelvic floor exercises, recovery timeline, pain tracking, education.

Vaginal or surgical recovery requires attention to pelvic floor health. This app provides exercises and tracking specifically for postpartum pelvic floor recovery—something rarely covered in general apps.

#3: Wysa — Mental Health & Postpartum Support

Wysa

Price: Free with premium | Features: AI mental health support, postpartum depression screening, journaling, crisis resources.

Postpartum depression and anxiety are real. Wysa provides mental health support with specific postpartum depression/anxiety screening and 24/7 accessible support when you need to talk.

#4: Soula — Postpartum Mental Health Specialist

Soula

Price: $9.99/month | Features: Postpartum mental health monitoring, AI chatbot, peer community, professional support connections.

Soula specializes in postpartum mental health, including depression, anxiety, and perinatal mood disorders. The app is built by maternal health experts specifically for the postpartum period.

#5: Ovia — Comprehensive Postpartum Tracking

Ovia

Price: Free with premium | Features: Postpartum recovery tracking, mood monitoring, physical symptom tracking, postpartum exercises, nutrition.

Ovia has a strong postpartum section covering physical recovery, mood changes, and exercises. Good if you were using Ovia during pregnancy and want continuity.

The Recommendation

For comprehensive mother + baby health: Wermom. The integration of postpartum recovery with baby health tracking means your healthcare picture is complete in one app.

For postpartum mental health focus: Soula + Wysa. Mental health is critical and shouldn't be secondary to baby tracking.

For physical recovery: Pelvic Floor First + your app of choice for overall health.

For continuity if you used Ovia in pregnancy: Stay with Ovia postpartum.
