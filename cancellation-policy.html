Cancellation Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026

1. Overview

Wermom is committed to offering a flexible cancellation policy that puts you in control. You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings without penalties or complicated procedures. We understand that circumstances change, and we want to make it as easy as possible for you to manage your subscription.

2. Free Trial Cancellation

Wermom offers a 7-day free trial to all new users. A valid payment method is required to sign up, but you will not be charged during the trial period if you cancel before it ends. To avoid any charges, you must cancel your subscription before your 7-day trial period expires. If you cancel during the trial, your card will not be charged and you will not be converted to a paid subscription.

3. Paid Subscription Cancellation

After your free trial period ends, your subscription automatically converts to a paid plan at $299 per year. You can cancel your paid subscription at any time through your account settings. When you cancel, your access to Wermom will continue until the end of your current billing period. After your billing period expires, you will no longer have access to the service. Please note that cancellation does not entitle you to any refunds for the time remaining in your billing period.

4. How to Cancel

Canceling your Wermom subscription is straightforward. Open the Wermom app on your device and navigate to Settings, then select Account. From there, choose Manage Subscription and follow the prompts to cancel your subscription. Alternatively, if you have any difficulties or prefer to cancel via email, you can contact our support team at support@wermom.com and we will process your cancellation request promptly.

5. Refund Policy

Wermom does not offer refunds after a payment has been processed. If you cancel your subscription during the free trial period, no charge will be applied to your payment method. However, once your paid subscription begins and you are charged, no refunds or credits will be issued, even if you cancel immediately after payment. We also do not offer prorated refunds for partial billing periods—once charged, the full annual subscription fee is non-refundable.

6. After Cancellation

After you cancel your subscription, you will retain access to Wermom until the end of your current billing period. Your health data will be retained for 30 days following your cancellation date. During this 30-day window, you can request a data export of all your information if you wish to keep a copy. After 30 days, all data associated with your account will be permanently deleted and cannot be recovered. We encourage you to export your data if you think you may want it in the future.

7. Reactivation

If you have canceled your Wermom subscription and wish to use the service again, you can reactivate your account at any time. When you resubscribe, a new subscription will begin with a fresh billing cycle. Please be aware that if your data was permanently deleted following your 30-day grace period after cancellation, it may not be recoverable. We recommend exporting your data before the 30-day window expires if you think you may want to reactivate in the future.

8. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns regarding cancellation, refunds, or any other matters related to this policy, please do not hesitate to reach out to our support team at support@wermom.com. We are here to assist you and will respond to your inquiries as quickly as possible.
